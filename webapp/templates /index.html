Project Overview

This project analyzes state-wise electricity consumption across India from January 2019 to December 5, 2020, covering the COVID-19 nationwide lockdown period. It looks at three scenarios: overall consumption trends, regional variation in demand, and the pace of recovery after lockdown restrictions eased.
